Hi, I'm San.

I currently teach myself front-end development while studying full time at a university. I love exploring ideas through art, and believe programming should not be an exception.

The way I see it, programming unlocks a whole new well of potential for artists. And it's unfortunate that not many pair it with art, as if they are mutually exclusive fields.

A program is no less than a brush in creating art that inspires. And I want to demonstrate it by making art out of it(literally). As someone who takes art seriously, I am also a die-hard optimist on the Ethereum network in the domain of NFTs.

Between visual arts and programming, it is my vision that I can get you — hopefully — to appreciate, or even possibly fall in love with, the union of the two.
